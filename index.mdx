Thirukkural.AI bridges the 2,000-year-old Tamil classic *Thirukkural* — 1,330 couplets of timeless wisdom on virtue, wealth, and love — with modern life through AI. Tell the platform how you feel, and it surfaces the Kural most relevant to your situation, along with bilingual Tamil and English text, thematic context, and actionable guidance.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the personality quiz">
    Answer 15 questions covering decision-making, values, and life philosophy. The platform scores your responses across five dimensions — wisdom, compassion, strength, harmony, and ambition — and assigns you a personality type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe how you feel">
    Type a free-form query ("I'm feeling overwhelmed at work") or pick from curated emotion suggestions. You can also hit **Surprise Me** for a random Kural matched to your personality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your Kural">
    The AI agent retrieves the most relevant verses from the dataset, then uses Google Vertex AI (Gemini) to compose a response with the Tamil original, English translation, thematic analysis, and practical guidance tailored to your personality.
  </Step>
</Steps>
