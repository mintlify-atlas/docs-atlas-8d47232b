## What is Thirukkural.AI?

The *Thirukkural* is a collection of 1,330 two-line couplets (*kurals*) written by the Tamil poet-philosopher Thiruvalluvar. Organized into three books — Virtue (*Aram*), Wealth (*Porul*), and Love (*Inbam*) — it covers topics ranging from ethics and governance to friendship, grief, and desire. Scholars regard it as one of the greatest works of world literature, yet its classical Tamil and aphoristic density make it difficult to engage with in daily life.

Thirukkural.AI closes that gap. You describe how you feel — in plain English or Tamil — and the platform retrieves the most relevant verse from the full corpus, then uses Google Vertex AI (Gemini) to explain the kural in the context of your specific situation, your personality type, and modern life.

## The problem it solves

Ancient wisdom texts are hard to access in two ways:

1. **Discovery** — You cannot browse 1,330 verses looking for one that matches your current emotion. The signal-to-noise ratio is too high.
2. **Interpretation** — Even when you find a relevant kural, the classical Tamil and terse couplet format require significant background to unpack into practical guidance.

Thirukkural.AI solves both problems. A custom retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipeline scores every verse against your query using keyword overlap, thematic matching, and sequence similarity. The top results are passed to Gemini, which synthesizes them into a personalized response with Tamil text, English translation, thematic analysis, and actionable guidance calibrated to your personality type.

## Personality types

Before you search, Thirukkural.AI asks you fifteen questions covering decision-making style, values, conflict response, and life philosophy. Your answers are scored across five dimensions — wisdom, compassion, strength, harmony, and ambition — and assigned to one of five personality archetypes.

    **Dominant dimension:** Wisdom

    **Traits:** Analytical, Ethical, Thoughtful

    **Traits:** Empathetic, Kind, Understanding
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The Strong Leader" icon="shield">
    **Dominant dimension:** Strength

    You embody courage, determination, and leadership. You stand firm in conflict, recover quickly from failure, and believe effort creates your path. The platform highlights kurals from the *Porul* book on governance, self-discipline, and overcoming adversity.

    **Traits:** Courageous, Determined, Leadership
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The Peaceful Soul" icon="leaf">
    **Dominant dimension:** Harmony

## Tech stack

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|------------|
| Frontend | Next.js 16 (React 19), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, Framer Motion |
| Backend | FastAPI (Python), Uvicorn |
| AI model | Google Vertex AI — Gemini (via `google-genai` SDK) |
| Retrieval | Custom RAG retriever (`UniqueRagKuralFetcher`) scoring against `kural_data_raw.json` |
| State | Browser `localStorage` for quiz answers and personality profile |

The frontend communicates with the backend through a single REST endpoint — `POST /api/v1/ask` — sending the user's question and personality profile, and receiving a structured response with a summary, a list of matching kurals, and actionable guidance.
